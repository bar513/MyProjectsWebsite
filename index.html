Clawy Arduino multy MCUswood working

Hi, meet Clawy! Clawy is a custom claw machine powered by three Arduino boards that control old prusa i3 motors, WS2812B LED strip, audio via a DFPlayer Mini, and all the gameplay logic. I designed a small custom board to safely drive the 48V claw mechanism, handling the higher power without frying the system. The frame's built from old pine wood — scrap from a broken bed and floorboards — rough but solid. And finally, I wrote some cool custom algorithms to make the game feel more skill-based, tense, and fun. Jump in to read more
